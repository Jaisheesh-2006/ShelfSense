# RISKS

> Known risks, unknowns, mitigations. Surfacing risks is part of the engineering judgment
> graded by the rubric. Facts in [[GROUND_TRUTH]]; pending decisions (PD-*) in [[DECISIONS]].

| ID | Risk | Impact | Likelihood | Mitigation |
|----|------|--------|------------|------------|
| R-1 | **Acceptance-gate failure** (`docker compose up` needs manual steps, `/metrics` missing, no DESIGN.md/CHOICES.md, crash) | **Rejected before scoring** | Med | Treat gate as P0: one-command compose, healthchecks, `/metrics` early, generate DESIGN/CHOICES (ADR-0003). |
| R-2 | **Integrity cap** — outputs hardcoded or invariant to input | **Score capped at 50** | Med | Everything computes from input; vary visibly with clip; no magic constants. |
| R-3 | **Window mismatch** (2-min video vs full-day CSV) handled naively | Misleading conversion; looks naive | High | Document assumption (A3); comparable-window or representative-sample approach (PD-3). |
| R-4 | Unknown which camera is entrance/checkout; views may not overlap | Wrong footfall & zones | ~~High~~ **Resolved** | Corrected dataset **names cameras by role** (`*-entry`, `*-zone`, `*-billing`); mapping is explicit ([[GROUND_TRUTH]] §1). |
| R-5 | Occlusion / crowding / group entry break tracking → ID switches | Bad footfall & sessions (Detection 30) | ~~High~~ **Largely mitigated for the dominant error** | **Measured + fixed where tractable:** the dominant overhead-CCTV errors were **Re-ID over-split** (same person front/back → many ids) and **group-merge** (2 people → 1 box). **ADR-0037** added per-camera **motion tracklet-stitching** that resolves the *within-camera* over-split (Store_2 ZONE staffer 4 ids → 1; footfall now matches GT). Remaining: **group-merge** (a detection limit, [[EDGE_CASES]] #9) and **cross-camera** staff duplication (appearance-bound, ADR-0036; a homography would fix it, deferred). Honest store-wide output is still a head-count band + per-camera figures ([[EDGE_CASES]] #7/#9, [[GROUND_TRUTH]] §1). |
| R-6 | Staff counted as customers (salespersons present all day) | Inflated footfall, wrong conversion | **Partly mitigated** | Exclude staff via **per-store uniform-colour** heuristic (black/pink) + optional **VLM** (ADR-0009/0032/0027). ⚠ Residual: overhead CCTV hides uniforms, so the split stays approximate ([[GROUND_TRUTH]] §1) — the total head-count is the more reliable output. |
| R-7 | Funnel double-counts (per-detection not per-session) | Loses the 35-mark bucket | Med | Session-based funnel, each session once per stage ([[BUSINESS_RULES]]). |
| R-8 | Streaming backbone setup friction for reviewers | Gate risk (R-1) | Med | **Resolved (ADR-0005):** no broker — the detector POSTs events to `/events/ingest` (idempotent); one-command compose. |
| R-9 | ffprobe/ffmpeg unavailable locally → unknown video specs | Slows zone/entry work | Low | Use OpenCV inside detector container; record specs in [[GROUND_TRUTH]]. |
| R-10 | Zone mapping from a plan rather than homography | Approximate zone assignment | Low | Per-store **layout PNGs** provided ([[GROUND_TRUTH]] §4); camera-level zones (PD-4). |
| R-11 | Over-engineering vs. timeframe | Incomplete end-to-end system | Med | Ship thin vertical slice first (CLAUDE.md Principle 1), then deepen ([[TASKS]]). |
| **R-12** | Schema ambiguity — PDF page-5 flat schema (built) vs. the richer `sample_events.jsonl` | Could emit the "wrong" schema vs. the held-out validator | ~~High~~ **Resolved (D1)** | User: **emit the flat page-5 schema only** ("clearly given your pipeline must emit this"); sample's extra signals not adopted ([[EVENT_SCHEMA]], ADR-0024). |
| **R-13** | POS loader breaks on the new 7-col CSV (parsed old GMV/20-col format) | Conversion & day-KPIs wrong/empty | ~~High~~ **Resolved (D3)** | `pos_loader.py` reworked: basket = distinct `order_time`, value = Σ `total_amount`, `brand` replaces `dep_name`; validated on the real CSV (₹34,331.71). |
| **R-14** | Store_2 unprocessed | Miss half the data / per-store metrics | ~~Med~~ **Resolved (ADR-0028/0030); accuracy-bounded (ADR-0036)** | Pluggable per-store registry; detector loops both stores tagging `store_id`. **ST1009** processed (calibrated lines, crowd-tuned, pink-staff hybrid). Reaches ~23 vs 25 GT, but **user crop-adjudication showed that's a coincidence** (over-split + group-merge + misses roughly cancel). Identities, not the headline count, are the weak link — see [[GROUND_TRUTH]] §1. |
| **R-15** | **Demographics from blurred faces** — sample implies gender/age but footage is full-face-blurred | Fabricated/unreliable predictions → integrity-cap risk | Med | Only emit if honestly derivable; otherwise document the limitation (D4) rather than guess. |
| **R-16** | **`assertions.py` / held-out tests unseen** — we self-validate | Our outputs may miss what the real scorer checks | Med | Treat `sample_events.jsonl` as the closest proxy; keep outputs schema-flexible; document assumptions. |
